# ProjexaLMS
The Project-First LMS for Modern Universities

ProjexaLMS is a modern, project-first Learning Management System designed to transform traditional classrooms into real-world, experiential learning environments.  
It enables universities to scale project-based, collaborative education across departments, research labs, and industry partnerships.  

Learn more at [projexa.ai](https://projexa.ai/)

---

## Key Features

- **Project Lifecycle Management**  
  Create, manage, and monitor projects with milestones, deliverables, and rubrics.

- **Collaboration Tools**  
  Form teams, assign roles, share files, and communicate seamlessly.

- **Faculty & Mentor Dashboards**  
  Gain real-time visibility into student contributions, submissions, and overall progress.

- **Structured Review & Feedback**  
  Enable accountability and critical thinking with customizable assessments.

- **Portfolio & Showcase**  
  Highlight outstanding student projects and showcase institutional achievements.

- **AI-Powered Insights**  
  Use advanced analytics for contribution tracking, code analysis, and intelligent project feedback.

---

## How It Works

1. **Setup a Call** – Consult with our team to configure the platform for your institution.  
2. **Onboard Users** – Add faculty, coordinators, and students with bulk import tools.  
3. **Launch Projects** – Create sessions and upload problem statements using customizable templates.  
4. **Track & Report** – Monitor progress and generate detailed reports from intuitive dashboards.
